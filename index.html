About Us

The Sustainable Waste Resource Recovery Lab is led by Principal Investigator Dr. Xavier Fonoll Almansa in the Fariborz Maseeh Department of Civil, Architectural and Environmental Engineering. We use microbial community engineering, environmental & cost analyses to develop sustainable chemical & biological processes that recover resources from wastes and remove emerging contaminants and pathogens. We collaborate with utilities, industry, national labs, and other academic institutions.
